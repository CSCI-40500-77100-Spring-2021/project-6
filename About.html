Who we are

Let’s face it – textbooks are expensive. A student enrolled in a four-year university spends on average $1,240 on books and supplies each year. Here at Maven, we want to make learning accessible to all, so you don’t feel like you are spending a small fortune on books you won’t need in a year. Maven is a decentralized library, where you can rent and trade textbooks you own with ones you need, at prices that you determine.

Interested? Then join a network of like-minded people that are helping each other save money all around the world.
